Astronomy

Introduction

Astronomy is the scientific study of celestial objects, space, and the universe as a whole. It focuses on understanding the origins, evolution, and physical properties of objects like stars, planets, galaxies, and black holes.

Celestial Mechanics: The study of how celestial bodies move and interact with each other, governed by the laws of gravity and physics.

Astrophysics: Understanding the physical and chemical properties of celestial objects and the processes that occur within them.
